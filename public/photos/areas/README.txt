Area photo naming convention:

- /photos/areas/{area-slug}/hero.jpg
- /photos/areas/{area-slug}/{service-slug}-after.jpg

Examples:

- /photos/areas/pottstown/hero.jpg
- /photos/areas/pottstown/mulching-after.jpg
- /photos/areas/collegeville/leaf-cleanup-after.jpg

Use real project photos where possible. Keep service slugs aligned with site URLs.
